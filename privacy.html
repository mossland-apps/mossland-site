Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

Mossland LLC develops and publishes mobile applications. This Privacy Policy describes how information is handled across our applications.

1. Information Collection

Each Mossland application is designed differently. The type of information collected, if any, depends on the specific application.

Some applications may:

  • Store data locally on your device
  • Use Google Play services for purchases
  • Integrate optional third-party services

If an application collects, processes, or shares personal data, those details are disclosed in that application's Google Play Store listing and data safety section.

If an application does not collect personal information, it will state so clearly in its listing.

2. Local Data Storage

Certain applications may store information locally on your device to enable core functionality (such as saved preferences or saved content). This data remains on your device unless otherwise stated.

3. In-App Purchases

Some applications may offer in-app purchases through Google Play Billing. All payment transactions are processed by Google and are subject to Google’s privacy policy.

Mossland LLC does not receive or store your payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

Applications may rely on Google Play Services or other third-party SDKs. These services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party services used by a specific application.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our applications are not specifically directed at children under 13 unless explicitly stated in the application’s listing.
