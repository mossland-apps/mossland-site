Privacy Policy – Party Food Calculator

Effective date: February 26, 2026

Party Food Calculator is designed to work without user accounts and does not collect or sell personal information.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data from users.

The app stores settings and saved entries locally on your device using Android DataStore. This information remains on your device and is not transmitted to Mossland LLC or any external servers.

If you choose to make a purchase, payments are processed securely through Google Play Billing. Google may process purchase-related data to complete the transaction and provide purchase history, account access, and refunds as applicable.

Mossland LLC does not receive or store your payment information.

4. Third-Party Services

Party Food Calculator uses the following third-party service:

We do not use analytics services or advertising SDKs, and we do not track users across apps or websites.
